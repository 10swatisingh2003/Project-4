Mensuration

Formula of Square
  • Area of Square = l2
  • Perimeter of Square = 4×l
Formula of Rectangle
  • Area of Rectangle = l×w
  • Perimeter of Rectangle = 2(l+w)
Formula of Circle
  • Area of Circle = πr2
  • Perimeter of Circle = 2πr
Formula of Parallelogram
  • Area of a Parallelogram = b×h
  • Perimeter of Parallelogram = 2(b+h)
